Recommended Approach

Flexible CMS build with room to scale

For most new business websites, WordPress is a practical choice because it is cost-effective, easy for clients to update, SEO-friendly, and suitable for future expansion.

✓Easy content editing after handoff
✓Expandable for blog, booking, eCommerce, or memberships
✓Lower maintenance cost compared with fully custom systems

Suggested Tech Stack

CMS: WordPress + Elementor

Forms: Contact form with email notification

SEO: On-page SEO setup and clean URLs

Security: Basic hardening, spam protection, backup plugin

Handoff: Source files, documentation, and update guide
